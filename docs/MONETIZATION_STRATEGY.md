## Executive Summary

**The Opportunity:** No Shopify app combines trade-in operations with membership loyalty. Furloop ($69-99/mo) handles buyback operations but NOT customer retention. Smile.io ($49-599/mo) handles loyalty but NOT trade-ins. TradeUp bridges this gap.

**The Strategy:** Undercut Furloop on price ($19 vs $69), differentiate on value (membership tiers + loyalty), and use freemium to build reviews fast.

**Target:** 100 paying customers at $40 ARPU = $4,000 MRR within 6 months.

### The Gap TradeUp Fills

**Key Insight:** A card shop could use Furloop AND TradeUp together. But we can win by offering BOTH operational simplicity AND loyalty features.

## Part 4: Conversion Optimization

### The Problem

**80% of free trial users never convert to paid.** (Source: Totango SaaS Metrics)

### The Solution: 5-Minute Onboarding

Research shows:
- 7-day trials convert at 40% vs 30% for 30-day
- Opt-in (no CC) converts 18-25%
- Complex apps get more pricing complaints

**Our onboarding must be DEAD SIMPLE:**

```
Step 1: Connect Shopify (OAuth - one click)
Step 2: Choose template (Bronze/Silver/Gold pre-configured)
Step 3: Preview member portal
Step 4: Go live

Total time: Under 5 minutes
```

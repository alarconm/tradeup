## App Description (Long - 2,800 characters max)

```
TradeUp is the complete loyalty and trade-in management solution built specifically for collectibles retailers. Whether you sell sports cards, Pokemon, Magic: The Gathering, comics, or other collectibles, TradeUp helps you turn one-time buyers into lifelong members.

**STORE CREDIT & TRADE-INS**
Accept trade-ins and instantly issue store credit using Shopify's native store credit system. Set custom cashback rates by product category, track item values, and flip inventory for profit with the Quick Flip feature.

**MEMBERSHIP TIERS**
Create Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum (or any custom tiers) with escalating benefits:
• Cashback multipliers on trade-ins
• Bonus points on purchases
• Exclusive perks and early access
• Monthly bonus credits for premium members

**LOYALTY POINTS**
Automatically award points when customers make purchases. Higher tiers earn more points per dollar. Customers can track their rewards in the customer account portal.

**DASHBOARD & REPORTING**
See your program's performance at a glance:
• Total members and growth trends
• Credit issued and redeemed
• Trade-in volume and revenue
• Bonus payouts and ROI

**CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE**
Your customers get a branded rewards page showing their tier, points balance, available rewards, and progress to the next level. Works seamlessly in their Shopify account.

**POS READY**
Look up members at point of sale for in-store transactions. Process trade-ins on the spot and issue credit immediately.

**BUILT FOR COLLECTIBLES**
TradeUp understands the collectibles business. Track individual items through trade-in, listing, and sale. Calculate Quick Flip profits. Handle the unique needs of card shops, comic stores, and hobby retailers.

**PLANS FOR EVERY SIZE**
• Free: Up to 50 members, 2 tiers
• Starter ($19/mo): 200 members, 3 tiers
• Growth ($49/mo): 1,000 members, 5 tiers
• Pro ($99/mo): Unlimited everything

Start with the free plan and upgrade as your membership program grows. No commitment, cancel anytime.

**QUICK SETUP**
Get started in minutes with pre-built tier templates. Choose 2-tier, 3-tier, or 5-tier setups and customize to match your brand. The guided onboarding walks you through every step.

Built by Cardflow Labs - we're collectibles retailers ourselves and built TradeUp to solve the problems we face every day.
```

## FAQ Section (for app listing)

**Q: Does TradeUp work with Shopify POS?**
A: Yes! TradeUp includes a POS extension for looking up members and their reward status at the register.

**Q: How does store credit work?**
A: TradeUp uses Shopify's native store credit system. When you issue credit, it goes directly to the customer's Shopify account and can be used at checkout.

**Q: Can I customize the membership tiers?**
A: Absolutely. Create your own tier names, set custom cashback rates, point multipliers, and perks for each level.

**Q: What happens to my data if I uninstall?**
A: Your data is retained for 30 days in case you reinstall. After 30 days, it's permanently deleted. You can request immediate deletion by contacting support.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**
A: No contracts. Cancel anytime. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

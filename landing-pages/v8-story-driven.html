Our Story

We got tired of software that didn't understand our business.

TradeUp wasn't built in a Silicon Valley office. It was built in the back of a card shop in Oregon City, Oregon.

It started with a problem

We're Mike and Brandon, owners of ORB Sports Cards. Every week, we'd watch customers walk in, sell us their cards, and never come back. They had no reason to.

GameStop has PowerUp Rewards. Best Buy has their membership. Why didn't card shops have something similar?

We looked for software to solve this. What we found were generic loyalty apps that didn't understand trade-ins, or expensive enterprise solutions that cost more than our rent.

We didn't want to build a tech company. We just wanted to solve our problem. But once we built it, other shop owners kept asking if they could use it too.

— Mike Alarcon, Co-Founder

So we built TradeUp. Not as a startup play, but as a tool we needed for our own shop. It worked so well that we decided to share it.

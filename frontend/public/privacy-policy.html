Effective Date: January 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Cardflow Labs ("we," "our," or "us") operates TradeUp, a Shopify application that provides store credit, loyalty rewards, and membership management services for Shopify merchants.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our TradeUp application. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from Shopify

When you install TradeUp, we access certain information from your Shopify store:

  • Store information (name, domain, email)
  • Customer data (names, emails, order history) for enrolled members
  • Product information for trade-in tracking
  • Order data for bonus calculations

2.2 Information You Provide

We collect information you directly provide:

  • Membership tier configurations
  • Trade-in batch details and notes
  • Account settings and preferences

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Usage data and analytics
  • Error logs for troubleshooting
  • API request metadata

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the TradeUp service
  • Process trade-ins and calculate Quick Flip bonuses
  • Manage membership programs and store credit
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

4.1 Service Providers

Third-party vendors who assist in operating our service (hosting, analytics, payment processing via Shopify Billing).

4.2 Legal Requirements

When required by law, subpoena, or legal process.

4.3 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Employee access restrictions

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Upon app uninstallation:

  • We retain data for 30 days to allow for reinstallation
  • After 30 days, data is permanently deleted
  • You may request immediate deletion by contacting us

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@cardflowlabs.com.

8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We process data based on legitimate business interests and your consent
  • We respond to data subject requests within 30 days
  • We support Shopify's GDPR webhooks for customer data requests

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising rights

10. Children's Privacy

TradeUp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for material changes
